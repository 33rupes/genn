Meet the Team

GeNN is maintained by genn-team.

Dr James Knight is a post-doctoral researcher, working on the Brains-on-Board project at the School of Engineering and Informatics at the University of Sussex
James Turner is a graduate student at the Centre for Computational Neuroscience and Robotics (CCNR) at the University of Sussex.
Prof Thomas Nowotny is a Professor of Informatics at the University of Sussex
